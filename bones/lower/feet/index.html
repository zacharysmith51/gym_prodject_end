the seven tarsal bones are

  • Calcaneus the largest bone in the foot, it is commonly referred to as the heel of the foot.
  • Talus this irregularly shaped bone creates the lower portion of the ankle joint
  • Cuboid this muli-faceted bone sits on the out side of the foot near the fifth phalanx
  • the cuneiforms these three small bones are cloest to the five metatarsal bones
  • Navicilar this bone sits beween the talus and cuneiforms

the five metatarsal bones create the body of the foot.
